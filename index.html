About Us

Our Journey

We started out as a production house in kolkat a. Under the name of calcutt a cineverse in 2019. Through our Journey of growth, we produced ad films and feature films. While gathering experience, we also met other filmmakers And artistes who strengthened us to grow stronger together. We were becoming larger than what we Had started as. So after 5 years, we have decided to take the next step in our evolution and spread out our Branches. As others joined the family, we naturally grew to become not just a company but a collective of artistic Minds and entrepreneurs. This collective is who we are now, cineverse collective.

Director

A passionate director and creative producer with 15+ years of directing experience has helmed successful advertising campaigns for 20+ brands and has worked closely with some of the finest and diverse filmmakers of the country. Singer

Tathagata Singha


A national award-winning filmmaker who passed out of the film and television institute of india, pune, specialising in film direction. His short films, during this time, have won him both national and international awards. He has been in the ad film world for the last 10 years (having been the associate of mr.
